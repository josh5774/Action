01 — Overview

Executive Summary

This app is a community platform connecting emerging film industry creatives with opportunities, education, and networking, while also offering a consumer-facing entertainment hub for film fans.

🎬 Application Required

Creators

Actors, writers, filmmakers, editors, cinematographers, etc. — not yet union (SAG/industry established).

  • Audition opportunities
  • Film collaborators
  • Industry education
  • Networking
  • Community exposure
🍿 Open Access

Entertainment Fans

Users who enjoy consuming film industry content, events, and community discussions.

  • Film industry news
  • Articles & interviews
  • Events & screenings
  • Community discussions

Core Value Proposition

For Creatives: "A private network where emerging film professionals can find collaborators, auditions, and real industry connections."
For Fans: "A curated hub for entertainment news, events, and access to the rising generation of filmmakers."

Monetization Streams

  • Premium memberships
  • Acting class partnerships
  • Event tickets
  • Featured project listings
  • Sponsored content

03 — Features

Core Features

1. Creator Profiles

Users create professional profiles with the following fields and features.

Profile Fields

  • Name & headshot
  • Location
  • Profession
  • Bio & reel
  • Resume & skills
  • Credits & portfolio
  • Social links

Profile Features

  • Follow other creators
  • Messaging (Phase 2)
  • Portfolio hosting
  • Filmography

2. Project Marketplace

Members can post and apply for projects across multiple formats.

Project Types

  • Short & independent films
  • Music videos
  • Student films
  • Web series

Listing Includes

  • Title & description
  • Roles available
  • Compensation (paid/unpaid)
  • Location & shoot dates
  • Audition instructions

Actor Actions

  • Apply directly
  • Upload audition tapes
  • Submit profile

3. Acting Classes / Education

  • Partnerships with coaches
  • Browse acting classes with reviews & ratings
  • Class schedule & location (in-person / online)
  • Possible revenue share model

4. Social Networking

Activity feed examples: "Josh just booked a role", "Sarah posted a new short film", "John attended Stella Adler workshop"

  • Follow members & view activity feed
  • See what others are working on

11 — Positioning

Biggest Competitive Advantage

The platform focuses on pre-union creatives, which currently lack a centralized network.

Existing platforms like Backstage and IMDb do parts of this, but none combine casting, networking, classes, events, and media into one ecosystem.
